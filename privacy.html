Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25.02.2026

CV Cümle Sihirbazı respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Account & Personal Information

The app does not require account creation and does not request personal details such as name, email address, phone number, or contact information.

2. User-Entered Text

When you type a CV sentence into the app, that text is processed to generate improved versions.

Data processed: The text voluntarily entered by you in the app.

The app does not create user profiles and does not attempt to link this text to your real-world identity.

3. AI Processing (Third-Party Service Provider)

To provide its core functionality, the app securely transmits the text you enter to OpenAI, L.L.C., an external AI processing service provider acting solely as a service provider.

In addition to the user-entered text, limited technical request data may be processed as part of standard internet communication, including:

  • A randomly generated device identifier (used to enforce usage limits and prevent abuse)
  • IP address visible to the server during request processing

This data is transmitted solely for the purpose of generating the requested CV suggestions. OpenAI processes the data only to return the requested output and does not receive it for independent advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes on behalf of this app.

The app does not use this data for advertising, marketing, profiling, cross-app tracking, or data brokerage.

4. User Permission (Consent)

Before any text is transmitted to the external AI processing service provider, the app displays a consent screen explaining this data transmission.

If you do not provide consent, your text will not be transmitted and the “Profesyonelleştir” feature will not function.

5. Data Retention

The app does not permanently store or maintain a history of the text you enter.

Text is processed temporarily to generate a response and is not stored in long-term databases by the app.

Technical request data such as IP address may be temporarily logged by hosting or infrastructure providers for security, debugging, or rate-limiting purposes, but user-entered text is not stored in server logs by the app.

6. On-Device Storage

The app stores limited technical data locally on your device to ensure proper functionality, including:

  • Daily usage limits / remaining credits
  • Premium purchase status
  • A randomly generated device identifier (created on-device)
  • Your AI processing consent preference
  • Whether you have viewed the in-app guide

This information remains stored only on your device and is not used to directly identify you.

7. Payments

In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple’s in-app purchase system. The app does not receive, process, or store payment card information.

8. Analytics & Tracking

The app does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

We do not use user data for tracking as defined by the App Store privacy guidelines.

9. Data Sharing

The app does not sell or trade personal data.

The only data sharing that occurs is the transmission of user-entered text and limited technical request data to OpenAI, L.L.C., strictly for the purpose of generating improved CV suggestions and only after user consent.
