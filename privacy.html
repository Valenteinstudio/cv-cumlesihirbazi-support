Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06.02.2026

CV Cümle Sihirbazı respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Data Collection

CV Cümle Sihirbazı does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or contact details.

2. User Input

Text entered by the user (e.g., CV sentences) is processed temporarily to generate improved versions.

This content is not permanently stored, logged, or associated with any user identity.

3. AI Processing

User-provided text is securely transmitted to a third-party AI service (OpenAI) solely for the purpose of generating improved results.

The data is processed anonymously and is not used for profiling, advertising, or tracking.

4. Local Storage

The application stores limited technical data locally on the device, including:

  • Daily usage limits
  • Premium purchase status

This information remains on the user’s device and is not used for identification purposes.

5. Payments

In-app purchases are handled securely by Apple and RevenueCat.

CV Cümle Sihirbazı does not have access to users’ payment details.

6. Analytics & Tracking

CV Cümle Sihirbazı does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, or tracking technologies.

7. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.
