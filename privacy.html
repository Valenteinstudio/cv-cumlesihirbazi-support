Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19.02.2026

CV Cümle Sihirbazı respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app, why it is processed, and how it is protected.

1. Data Collection

CV Cümle Sihirbazı does not require an account and does not ask for personal details such as name, email address, phone number, or contact information.

2. User Input (Text You Enter)

When you enter text (for example, a CV sentence), the app processes that text to generate improved versions.

What is processed: the text you type into the app.

3. Third-Party AI Processing

To generate results, the text you provide is securely transmitted to a third-party AI service provider for processing.

The text is used only to produce the requested output and is not used by the app for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

Important: The app does not request or require you to include sensitive personal information in your text. Please avoid entering personal identifiers (e.g., ID numbers) in your CV sentence.

4. Permission / Consent

Before any text is sent to the third-party AI service provider, the app asks for your permission (one-time consent).

If you do not give permission, the “Profesyonelleştir” feature will not send your text and will not work.

5. Data Retention

The app does not permanently store or log the text you enter. The text is processed to generate a response and is not saved in the app as a history.

6. Local Storage (On-Device Only)

The app stores limited technical information locally on your device to function properly, such as:

  • Daily usage limits / remaining credits
  • Premium status (to unlock unlimited usage)
  • A random device identifier (generated on-device) to support usage limits
  • Whether you have seen the in-app guide
  • Your consent choice (to remember your permission)

This information stays on your device and is not used to identify you.

7. Payments

In-app purchases are processed securely by Apple’s in-app purchase system. The app does not receive or store your payment card details.

8. Analytics & Tracking

CV Cümle Sihirbazı does not use advertising networks and does not perform cross-app tracking.

9. Data Sharing

The app does not sell your personal data.

The app shares your text input only when necessary to provide the core feature: generating improved CV sentences via a third-party AI service provider (after you give consent).
