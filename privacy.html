Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19.02.2026

CV Cümle Sihirbazı respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Account & Personal Information

The app does not require account creation and does not request personal details such as name, email address, phone number, or contact information.

2. User-Entered Text

When you type a CV sentence into the app, that text is processed to generate improved versions.

Data processed: Only the text voluntarily entered by you in the app.

3. AI Processing (Third-Party Service)

To provide its core functionality, the app securely transmits the text you enter to OpenAI, L.L.C., an external AI processing service provider.

The transmitted data includes only the text you voluntarily enter in the app. No name, email address, payment information, contact details, or direct personal identifiers are transmitted for AI processing.

The transmitted text is used solely to generate the requested CV suggestions. It is not used by this app for advertising, profiling, marketing, or tracking purposes.

4. User Permission (Consent)

Before any text is transmitted to the external AI processing service provider, the app displays a one-time consent screen explaining this data transmission.

If you do not provide consent, your text will not be transmitted and the “Profesyonelleştir” feature will not function.

5. Data Retention

The app does not permanently store, log, or maintain a history of the text you enter. Text is processed temporarily to generate a response and is not saved within the app.

6. On-Device Storage

The app stores limited technical data locally on your device to ensure proper functionality, including:

  • Daily usage limits / remaining credits
  • Premium purchase status
  • A randomly generated device identifier (created on-device) to support usage limits
  • Your consent preference
  • Whether you have viewed the in-app guide

This information remains stored only on your device and is not used to directly identify you.

7. Payments

In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple’s in-app purchase system. The app does not receive, process, or store payment card information.

8. Analytics & Tracking

The app does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

9. Data Sharing

The app does not sell or trade personal data.

The only data sharing that occurs is the transmission of user-entered text to the external AI processing service provider, strictly for the purpose of generating improved CV suggestions, and only after user consent.
