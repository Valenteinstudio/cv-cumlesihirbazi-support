Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app work?
CV Cümle Sihirbazı transforms simple CV sentences into more professional and structured versions using an AI-powered text processing service.

What data is sent to generate results?
When you use the “Profesyonelleştir” feature, the text you enter is securely transmitted to OpenAI, L.L.C. for processing. Technical request data such as a randomly generated device identifier and IP address may also be processed as part of standard internet communication and service security.

Does the app store my text?
No. The app does not maintain a history of the text you enter. Text is processed temporarily to generate a response and is not stored in long-term databases by the app.

Is my data used for advertising or tracking?
No. The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking technologies, or data brokers. User data is not used for marketing or profiling.

How do payments work?
Premium access is offered as an in-app purchase. Purchases are securely handled by Apple’s in-app purchase system. The app does not receive or store payment card information.

I purchased Premium but it’s not active.
Please try “Restore Purchases” from the Premium screen. If the issue continues, contact us and include your device model and iOS version.
