FAQ

How does CV Cümle Sihirbazı work?
The app turns simple CV sentences into more professional versions. Your text is processed to generate results.

Does the app store my text?
No. The app does not keep a history of what you type. Text is processed to generate an output and is not stored in the app.

What data is sent to generate results?
Only the text you type into the app is sent for processing. The app does not require an account.

How do payments work?
Premium access is offered as an in-app purchase. Purchases are handled by the platform’s in-app purchase system. The app does not receive or store payment card details.

I purchased Premium but it’s not active.
Try “Restore Purchases” from the Premium screen. If the issue continues, email us and include your device model and iOS version.
